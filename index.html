  • What is powerlifting?
    Powerlifting is a sport that utlizes in three lifts bench press, squat, and deadlift to measure the athlete's absolute strength.
  • How can you get better at powerlifting?
    • Step 1: Be consistent, follow your program and make sure you do the amount of reps it tells you to do.
    • Step 2: Stretch properly!!!! Make sure each part of your body is well stretched and good before you start going into your lifts. Since you do not want your body to be as stiff as you are about to start.
    • Step 3: Make sure you are hydrated, hydration is key. Get enough electrolytes into your body so you are able to keep going in your workout as it will get challenging as time goes on.
    • Step 4: MOST IMPORTANTLY, RECOVER! You need your food and rest so your body can handle all of the lifting you did for the day and you will get stronger.
  • What is bodybuilding?
    Bodybuilding is the practice of developing the body through a regimen of exercises and build on muscle through hypertrophy.
  • Why is bodybuilding popular?
    Bodybuilding is popular due to its promotion of physical fitness and health, offering both aesthetic and mental benefits. The sport provides a sense of personal achievement and fosters a supportive community. Media influence, role models, and competitive elements also play significant roles in its widespread appeal, as does the potential for career opportunities in the fitness industry.
  • How to succeed in bodybuilding?
    To succeed in bodybuilding, set clear goals, follow a structured workout and nutrition plan, and maintain consistency in both training and diet. Prioritize adequate rest, monitor your progress, and continually educate yourself on effective techniques. Seek support from a community or coach and stay mentally resilient to overcome setbacks.
  • What kind of foods do you need to eat for bodybuilding?
    For bodybuilding, follow a balanced diet high in protein, complex carbohydrates, and healthy fats to support muscle growth and overall health. Eat frequent, nutrient-dense meals and stay well-hydrated. Consider nutrient timing and adjust caloric intake based on whether you're bulking (caloric surplus) or cutting (caloric deficit).
  • What kind of foods do you need to eat for powerlifting?
    For powerlifting, follow a diet rich in protein to support muscle repair and strength, along with sufficient complex carbohydrates and healthy fats for sustained energy and overall health. Eat frequent, balanced meals to maintain energy levels and optimize performance. Ensure adequate hydration and consider nutrient timing, particularly around training sessions, to enhance recovery and strength gains.
