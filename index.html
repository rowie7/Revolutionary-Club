  • What is powerlifting?
    Powerlifting is a sport that utlizes in three lifts bench press, squat, and deadlift to measure the athlete's absolute strength.
  • How can you get better at powerlifting?
    • Step 1: Be consistent, follow your program and make sure you do the amount of reps it tells you to do.
    • Step 2: Stretch properly!!!! Make sure each part of your body is well stretched and good before you start going into your lifts. Since you do not want your body to be as stiff as you are about to start.
    • Step 3: Make sure you are hydrated, hydration is key. Get enough electrolytes into your body so you are able to keep going in your workout as it will get challenging as time goes on.
    • Step 4: MOST IMPORTANTLY, RECOVER! You need your food and rest so your body can handle all of the lifting you did for the day and you will get stronger.
